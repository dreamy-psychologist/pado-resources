💡 Why does 30 seconds work?

The stress hormone cortisol peaks within 90 seconds. What you do during those 90 seconds matters.

A short break isn't about "resting" — it's about resetting your nervous system. You're gently pressing the brake (vagus nerve) of your autonomic nervous system.

💛 Good to Know

1

A micro break isn't about 'resting' — it's about resetting. You come back in a better state after 30 seconds.

2

You don't have to do it perfectly. Even one breath or one shoulder shrug sends a signal to your nervous system.

3

Frequent is better than long. Several short breaks throughout the day is best.

4

Your body remembers. With practice, you'll enter reset mode faster and faster.
